Manhole Covers

Networks of cables reside underground us, connecting our devices and allowing us to communicate information thrpough these networks. Manhole covers are often openings to these underground networks.

Cameras

Cameras are very present in the Wellington environment, with many purposes such as security and traffic managment. These cameras send this visual information to wider networks to be used and evaluated.

Power Lines

Power lines play a crucial part in our day to day lives, transferring electricity to power devices that we use to connect to the internet.
